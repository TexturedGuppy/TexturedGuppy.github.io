Hobo Hotbox Soccer

This is Hobo Hotbox Soccer! Final Project as a group with James Keeling and Cory Brown. Cory did all of the art assets for our game while James and I did all of the gameplay programming for the game. The game is a small sports game where two Hobos battle it out in a back alley somewhere to a friendly game of soccer. There are drugs used for power ups to give the players various powerups. Game has fully customizable controls, volume, and resolution through the menu.
